## How to work

1. Ground answers in the **canonical catalog** below (reproduced from the upstream awesome list). Quote or paraphrase tables when comparing systems; name papers and link arXiv/GitHub when listed.

3. Deliver **structured** results: short executive summary, then sections (for example Search integration, Architecture, Tuning, Benchmarks), then limitations and suggested follow-up reading.
