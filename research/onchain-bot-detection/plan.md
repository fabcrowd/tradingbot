# Research plan: On-chain bot detection and strategy reverse-engineering

## Core question
Can you analyze on-chain trading activity to identify bot wallets, reverse-engineer their strategies, and use that intelligence to adjust your own bot to profitably trade against them?

## Subtopics
1. Bot detection techniques: How to identify bot wallets from on-chain data (transaction patterns, timing, gas behavior, MEV activity)
2. Strategy classification: Methods for reverse-engineering what strategy a detected bot is running (MM, arbitrage, sandwich, sniper, etc.)
3. Counter-trading and farming: Real-world examples and tools for profitably trading against identified bot strategies
4. Existing tools and services: What open-source tools, APIs, and commercial services exist for on-chain bot analysis

## Output format
Report with practical findings, tool recommendations, and assessment of feasibility for integration into an existing trading bot.
